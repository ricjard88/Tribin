Our Journey

At Tribin, we proudly built a community experience platform in the Bay Area, curating local adventures with neighborhood businesses as event hubs. We enjoyed serving thousands of customers, hosting 276 unique experiences—including Airbnb's entire 2023 Christmas Party—and engaging an average of 350 participants weekly. With a network of 5 partner hubs and over 80 local artists, we proudly created a vibrant community around local culture.

A Fond Farewell

After validating product-market fit, we made the difficult decision to wind down Tribin due to insufficient long term revenue traction. We're grateful for the journey and the community we built.
